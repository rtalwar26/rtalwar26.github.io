Protecting your privacy online.

Ever wondered how internet advertisments are able to read your mind. In this talk I am going to explain you how internet giants like facebook/amazon/google are keeping a watch on you always . The talk will also explore ways to intelligently use these websites/apps so that your privacy is not compromised

No coding/programming background is required to attend this talk.

What you will take away :

  • Learn how internt companies track and learn about you.
  • How companies build an invisible trap around you.
  • How to use less known tools and applications to protect your privacy.
  • Simple habits to strengthen data privacy
  • The truth behind free and paid applications.

Outline/Structure of the talk

  1. Why there is an urgent need to save your privacy.
  2. How big companies are making money on your privacy.
  3. An example to show how a particular website is tracking you.
  4. Your phone number is enough to know about your Email addresses, Geographic locations, Names, Professional skills, Usernames, Years of professional experience.
  5. How to use modern tools to avoid leaking your privacy to internet companies.
  6. Simple tips to avoid getting trapped by emails/SMS.
  7. A live example to show how I can hack your password.

Target Audience

  • Smart phone Users
  • Internet Users
  • IT professionals
