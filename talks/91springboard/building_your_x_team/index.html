building your x team

This talk will explore the less known tips/tricks/hacks to get your startup in good shape and speed. Ill be sharing my learnings from working in worlds biggest startup - Amazon , to , working in other smaller startups and founding some of them on my own. The talk will revolve around company culture, communication techniques, handling code debt and how to execute your idea in a lean way.

No coding/programming background is required to attend this talk.

What you will take away :

  • Learn how to create a highly agile startup without Bosses and Titles
  • Learn how to avoid communication gaps between your team
  • How to make life of yourself and your programmers fun,light-weight without sacrificing speedy deliveries
  • How to discover free and open-source tools and integrate them with yours.
  • Why you might not need an app for your startup
  • How over-hyped terms like AI and blockchain break your confidence
  • How to turn every member of your team into a ninja, and hence an e(X)treme-team

Outline/Structure of the talk

  1. Why there is an urgent need to have X-teams.
  2. The art of failing fast and iterating faster.
  3. An awesome hack to clearly communicate your ideas to other team members and make sure everybody is walking together and in the same direction.
  4. The beauty of automating everything within your startup.
  5. Building on what others are building, make use of startup ecosystem.
  6. Fake it till you make it. Validation at every step.
  7. How to not get trapped into bubbles.
  8. Extreme programming tips , tricks, hacks.

  • Designers
